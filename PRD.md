# Idea2Sale: AI-Powered Marketing Hub PRD

## 1. Project Overview
Idea2Sale is a comprehensive AI marketing automation platform designed for e-commerce sellers on TikTok, Facebook, and Instagram. It transforms raw product ideas into high-converting campaigns through automated content creation, strategic planning, and performance analysis.

## 2. Target Audience
*   **Social Commerce Sellers**: Individual entrepreneurs selling on TikTok Shop/FB Marketplace.
*   **Digital Marketers**: Teams needing to scale content production.
*   **Brand Owners**: Users looking for data-driven product research and competitor analysis.

## 3. Functional Requirements

### A. Creative Studio (Restructured)
*   **AI Copywriter**: Generates Sales Page content, TikTok Scripts, and SEO keywords using high-converting marketing frameworks.
*   **Visual Assets**: Poster generation (GenAI) and Short Video Creation (VEO/AI Video).
*   **Smart Captioning**: Automatic hashtag and caption generation tailored to current trends.

### B. Smart Integration (TikTok/FB/IG)
*   **Auto-Post**: Integration with TikTok Content Posting API.
*   **Automation Hub**: Automated comment replies and inbox management (Auto-Responder).
*   **Status Tracking**: Real-time Firestore tracking for all published content.

### C. Strategy & Strategy (New)
*   **AI Product Research**: Analyze trending items on TikTok Creative Center and competitor pricing/strategy.
*   **Ads Manager Lite**: Simplified interface for defining "Target Personas" and generating ad sets.

### D. AI Planner
*   **Integrated Calendar**: Drag-and-drop posting schedule.
*   **Optimal Timing**: AI suggestions for best posting hours based on platform analytics.

## 4. User Workflow
1.  **Research**: Use AI Product Research to find a winning product.
2.  **Create**: Use Copywriter and Video gen to build high-quality assets.
3.  **Automate**: Set up Auto-Post and Auto-Response rules.
4.  **Promote**: Launch Ads Manager Lite to scale reach.
5.  **Analyze**: Monitor results via TikTok Analytics and the Planner.

## 5. UI/UX Vision
*   **sidebar**: Modern, categorized navigation with brand-consistent iconography.
*   **Theme**: "Modern Tech" aesthetic with glassmorphism and subtle brand accents.
